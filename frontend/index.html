Welcome to the RDE Influenza Dashboard

Fall 2024 RDE Systems NJIT Capstone

This project presents crucial data from the CDC regarding the Flu Vaccine, aiming to provide clear insights into vaccination trends. Developed by a team of seniors at the New Jersey Institute of Technology, this platform is the amalgamation of their years of study and hands-on experience in development.

The goal is to make this data accessible and understandable for all, from health professionals to the general public. By leveraging advanced data visualization techniques, we aim to shed light on key vaccination metrics, trends over time, and other relevant health data.

This initiative has been made possible through collaboration with RDE Systems, whose support has helped turn our vision into reality. Together, we are committed to contributing to the ongoing conversation about public health and preventive care.
